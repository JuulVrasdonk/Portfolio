Projectdetails

Stuweb is a project I did in the summer break. In this project I worked with friends and great designers, Daan Ketelaars and Tristan Varewijck. We wanted to design a platform on which fellow design students would be to find work in the webdesign discipline. We wanted to give Stuweb a very transparent look and feeling.

Text in this project is in Dutch.
