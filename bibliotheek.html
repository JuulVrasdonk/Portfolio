Projectdetails

This is a project I made for the school subject Visual Interface Design, in my first year of Communication and Multimedia Design. The challenge was to make an Ipad app, that would help High School students to find the book they need. We had to use style of 'de Bibliotheek' and change it to be pleasing to the eye of a High School student.

Text in this project is in Dutch.
